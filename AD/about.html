ADAS-viewer : Visualization of Alzheimer's Disease Alternative Splicing

 

ADAS-viewer is a web-based application providing a platform of transcript isoform-driven information in multiple brain regions.

It visualizes transcript-level molecular information and provides a tool to identify its clinical associations.

One can analyze transcript and AS patterns with clinical data.
